About AJ Carpentry

Built on Skill,
Backed by Trust

AJ Carpentry is a family-run business with over two decades of experience delivering bespoke joinery and carpentry solutions across North London and the surrounding areas.

Every project — whether a set of fitted wardrobes or a full kitchen installation — receives the same dedication to quality. We work closely with you from initial consultation through to installation, ensuring the finished result exceeds your expectations.

We take pride in using only the finest materials, combined with traditional craftsmanship and modern techniques to create pieces that are built to last a lifetime.
