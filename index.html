Our Story

A Little Corner of Greece in Your City

GREKO was born from a simple dream — to share the warmth and flavour of a real Greek kitchen. Our founder Nikos left Thessaloniki with his grandmother's recipes and a passion for honest, delicious food.

Everything we serve is made fresh each morning: hand-rolled spanakopita, slow-marinated chicken souvlaki, creamy homemade tzatziki, and imported deli goods you won't find anywhere else.
